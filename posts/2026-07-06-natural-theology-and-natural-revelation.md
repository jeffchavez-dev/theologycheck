One of the critiques of non-Classicalists is to argue that we confuse Natural Theology and Natural/General Revelation.

Natural theology, on the other hand, is the interpretation and systemization of the data of natural revelation. In general, early modern Reformed theologians employed natural theology to varying degrees in their theology, which represents a continuity with the theologians of the patristic era and the Middle Ages. – Vos, Geerhardus. Natural Theology. Edited and translated by Albert Gootjes. Grand Rapids, MI: Reformation Heritage Books, 2022.
